FAQ

Got Questions?

How much ad spend do I need on top of the package fee? ▾

We recommend a minimum of ₹5,000–₹10,000/month ad spend for Soundcheck, and ₹15,000+ for higher tiers. The ad spend goes directly to Meta/YouTube — we manage it transparently and you own the accounts.

Do I keep my ad accounts if I leave? ▾

Absolutely. Everything is set up under your business — ad accounts, pixels, audiences. It's yours forever. We believe in building assets for our artists, not locking them in.

Can you guarantee streams or followers? ▾

We don't guarantee vanity numbers because that's not how real marketing works. What we guarantee is strategic, data-driven campaigns that put your music in front of the right people. Real growth takes real effort — we bring the system, you bring the music.

I'm a complete beginner. Is Soundcheck right for me? ▾

Yes! Soundcheck is specifically designed for artists who have never run paid campaigns. We set up everything from scratch — your ad accounts, tracking, audiences — and launch your first campaigns. You'll go from zero infrastructure to running ads within a week.

What genres do you work with? ▾

All of them. Hip-hop, EDM, pop, indie, Punjabi, classical fusion, Bollywood — genre doesn't matter. Marketing principles are universal. We adapt the targeting and creative strategy to match your audience.

How long until I see results? ▾

You'll see ad data within 48 hours of launch. Meaningful traction typically builds over 4-8 weeks as we optimize targeting and creatives. That's why we require a 3-month minimum — it takes time to build a real growth engine.
